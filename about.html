About Sommeliers Academy Sweden

Sommeliers Academy Sweden provides structured and internationally aligned sommelier education within the Swedish hospitality landscape.

Our programmes combine academic wine theory, disciplined blind tasting methodology, and professional service standards rooted in real restaurant environments.

The academy serves aspiring sommeliers, hospitality professionals, restaurant teams, and dedicated wine enthusiasts seeking structured and rigorous development.

Based in Gothenburg and expanding beyond, we aim to elevate the standard of wine education in Sweden through precision, mentorship, and international relevance.
