About Sommeliers Academy Sweden

Structured, internationally aligned education built for real hospitality.

Founded in 2026 in Gothenburg, Sommeliers Academy Sweden was established to provide structured and internationally aligned sommelier education within the Swedish hospitality landscape.

Our programmes combine academic wine theory, disciplined blind tasting methodology, and professional service standards rooted in real restaurant environments.

The academy serves aspiring sommeliers, hospitality professionals, restaurant teams, and serious wine students seeking rigorous and practical development.

We believe wine education should be precise, structured, and internationally relevant — preparing students not only for examinations, but for professional excellence.
