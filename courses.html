Certificate in Sommellerie & Service — Level 1

40h on campus • 20h+ independent study

A comprehensive foundation in viticulture, winemaking, grape varieties, regions, structured tasting, and professional service.

Delivery

  • In-person seminars, tutored tastings, and service workshops
  • Applied food and wine pairing exercises
  • Certified sommeliers and wine educators
  • Designed for readiness for ASI Prep examination

Core areas of study

  • Vineyard and grape science
  • Winemaking, ageing, and faults
  • Principal white and red grape varieties
  • Appellations and label interpretation
  • Sparkling, fortified, and sweet wines
  • Professional wine service and guest interaction
  • Beer, spirits, tea, coffee, and sake

Assessment

  • Continuous assessment
  • Portfolio work
  • Practical service exam
  • Final written exam
  • Blind tasting

Advanced Certificate in Professional Sommellerie — Level 2

Aligned to ASI Certification 1

An advanced programme focused on benchmark wines, precision tasting, fine-dining service mastery, and beverage list strategy.

Prerequisites and structure

  • Level 1 or validated equivalent experience
  • 30+ hours mandatory online pre-course theory
  • 40 hours face-to-face advanced instruction
  • Blind tasting training with a minimum of 24 wines

Advanced focus areas

  • Global viticulture and advanced winemaking
  • Benchmark white and red wines
  • Advanced blind tasting in ASI format
  • Fine-dining service under exam conditions
  • Food pairing at professional level
  • List costing, cellar management, and leadership

Assessment

  • Professional practical sommelier exam
  • Blind tasting exam
  • Final written theory assessment
  • Food and wine pairing oral defense
